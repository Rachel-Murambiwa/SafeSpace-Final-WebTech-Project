About SafeSpace

Our Mission

SafeSpace exists to create a gentle, anonymous community where women can share emotional experiences and find compassionate responses. We prioritize privacy, accessibility, and non-judgment.

Privacy Pledge

We encourage the use of pseudonyms. The platform is built to minimize personal data collection. (Note: this demo uses localStorage for accounts — please replace with a secure backend for production.)
